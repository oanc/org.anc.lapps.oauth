OAuth Client Authorization

Use this form to obtain an OAuth token that can be used to retrieve Gigaword documents from the Linguistic Data Consortium (LDC). When you click the authorize button you will be redirected to the LDC login form. When your LDC credentials have been verified by the LDC you will be redirected back here with a OAuth token that is valid for one hour. Copy the access token and paste it into applications (e.g. http://galaxy.lappsgrid.org) to retreive documents from the LDC.

NOTE: Even with a valid OAuth token, you will not be able to retreive documents from the LDC if you, or your institution, has not purchased access to the resource you are trying to use.
